🕵 Recon & Intelligence

Perform passive and active intelligence gathering on target hosts and networks.

  • Reverse DNS / ASN Lookup – Maps IPs to hostnames and autonomous systems.
  • WHOIS Lookup – Retrieves registrar and ownership data.
  • IP Range Scanner – Sweeps and scans CIDR blocks for open services.
  • Real IP Resolver – Attempts to bypass proxy/CDN and locate origin IP.
